# Scenes Folder Overview

This folder contains all the **Unity scene files** used in the **Driving Education Simulation**. Each scene represents a distinct part of the simulation, including gameplay, mini-games, and interface navigation.

---

## Level1Part1Unity
- This is the **main Level 1 gameplay scene** used in Unity.  
- The scene covers the **entire Level 1 experience**, including:  
  - Player entering and controlling the car  
  - Interactive mini-games, such as the vehicle inspection  
  - Driving through roads with traffic and hazards  
  - Completing the lesson with the parking activity  
- All interactions, UI, and level mechanics for Level 1 are handled in this scene.

---

## MainMenuPart3Unity
- This scene is used for the **Main Menu interface** and navigation.  
- Includes:  
  - Main menu panels and buttons  
  - Game settings interface  
  - Access to the **car inspection mini-game**  
  - Level selection and scene switching controls  
- All menu interactions, audio feedback, and UI transitions are managed in this scene.

---

This structure ensures that each Unity scene is clearly organized for its specific role in the simulation and can be easily located and edited for development or review.
